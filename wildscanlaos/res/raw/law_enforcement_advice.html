Always follow agency protocol when dealing with trafficked animals. Endangered species traffickers will go to great lengths to smuggle wildlife including forging documents, smuggling protected species with non-protected species, sedating animals and disguising animals and their part or derivatives to appear to be legal. Ensure all papers purporting to allow trade such as CITES permits are examined thoroughly and that any investigative leads noted and pursued. The ASEAN Wildlife Enforcement Network and other regional platforms should be utilized and informed when trafficking is found to be transnational.
